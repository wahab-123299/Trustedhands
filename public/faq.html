Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions from our customers and artisans.

For Customers

How do I book an artisan?

Search for your desired service, view artisan profiles, and click “Book Now” to hire.

How do I know if an artisan is skilled?

Check the artisan’s profile for verified badges, reviews, and completed job ratings.

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes, you can cancel a booking before the artisan accepts it by visiting your user dashboard.

For Artisans

How do I register as an artisan?

Go to the Artisan Register page, fill out your information, and upload verification documents.

Do I pay to join Trusted Hand?

Registration is free. A small service fee applies only when you successfully complete a job.

How do I get more jobs?

Maintain good ratings, complete your profile, and request customer feedback to build trust.
